Game preservation is at risk!

RetroDECK is committed to preserving retro and vintage games for all. However, even new games are at risk when online components are shut down. If you are an EU citizen you can help us in our mission by signing the Stop Killing Games EU initiative!

Your all-in-one emulation package for the Steam Deck

Powered by ES-DE, RetroDECK combines all of the best open-source emulators into a single application. Pair that with a best-in-class Steam Deck controller configuration, and you'll be in retro gaming paradise.

Get it on
Flathub

No need to configure emulators

(But you can if you want)

With RetroDECK, everything has been pre-configured for optimal performance and quality on the Steam Deck. If you want to dig deeper, you tinker with common emulator settings in the RetroDECK Configurator. And don’t worry – you can always reset with a single click.

Designed for Gaming Mode

RetroDECK fits right in with the other games in your Steam library.

Custom ES-DE Theme

RetroDECK includes a custom ES-DE theme with pixel text and retro vibes. But you can also choose any ES-DE theme in the settings menu. Thank you to Weestuarty for their amazing work!
